Jekyllization

Jekyll

I’ve been reading about Jekyll (on github) and it looks like a much more desirable way to host a website and related blog than wordpress. It works great because all your posts are stored as text using haml and kept under source control – It’s makes it easy to work from anywhere, and gives you feature partiy you could get with MarsEdit – but just easier to work with.

It’s so easy – it looks like stealing candy from a baby! – What a lark, let’s try!

Concentration and simplynoise.com

I’m a fan of using white noise - mainly from using a floor fan in my bedroom on summer nights to drown out the noisy streets. It drowns out any noise wafting up from 35th street - and there’s plenty of noise on that street.

It turns out I’m also a big fan of white noise when working - or my favorite brown noise from simplynoise.com. Combine that and the ticking of a pomodoro and my ADHDness melts away.
